A field of 36 letters will appear on the screen within the module. Some of these letters will spell up to three words from the charts below. Words may be spelled forward or backward.

  1. Use the last digit of the serial number (even or odd) to determine the correct words in the chart to reference.
  2. Read the letters in the corners of the field. For each letter, locate the word in the chart whose box shows that letter in the same corner as its position in the field.
  3. Only one of these words will be found in the field. Locate and select the first and last letter in the word to disarm the module. The bomb will record a strike if any incorrect letters are pressed.
